About Us

Kerala Kala Kendram, Margao is a vibrant socio-cultural organisation dedicated to promoting the rich heritage, art, and traditions of Kerala among Keralites residing in Goa. We organize festivals, traditional performances, cultural workshops, and community welfare activities.
