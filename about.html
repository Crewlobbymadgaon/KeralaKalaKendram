About Us

Kerala Kala Kendram, Margao is a socio-cultural organization founded to uphold and promote the rich traditions, values, and cultural heritage of Kerala among the Malayalee community residing in Goa.

Our mission is to bring together families through cultural programs, festivals, educational initiatives, and social support, fostering unity and pride in our identity.

The organization conducts various events including Onam celebrations, arts workshops, language classes, and youth programs to encourage cultural participation and harmony.
