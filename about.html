About Us

Kerala Kala Kendram, Margao is a socio-cultural organization founded to uphold and promote the rich traditions, values, and cultural heritage of Kerala among the Malayalee community residing in Goa.

Our mission is to bring together families through cultural programs, festivals, educational initiatives, and social support‚Äîfostering unity, belonging, and pride in our shared identity.

The Kendram conducts a wide range of events and initiatives, including Onam celebrations, arts workshops, language classes, and youth engagement programs, to encourage active cultural participation and community harmony.

As an active partner of the Malayalam Mission, we conduct Malayalam language classes to help the younger generation connect with their linguistic roots. We also offer regular Bharatanatyam, drawing, and other cultural classes to nurture creativity and young talent.

Our vibrant Onam celebrations‚Äîfeaturing traditional games, performances, and the grand Onasadya‚Äîbring the community together in the true spirit of Kerala. We also host Sahitya Koottaymas (literary gatherings) to promote creative expression and a love for Malayalam literature.

Kerala Kala Kendram is deeply committed to community welfare, providing financial aid and resources during times of natural disasters and emergencies. We also recognize and honor meritorious students and achievers within our community.

More than an organization, Kerala Kala Kendram is a cultural family‚Äîwelcoming all Malayalees in Goa and working tirelessly to preserve our heritage, support one another, and strengthen the bonds that unite us.
